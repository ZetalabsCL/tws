Our History

TWS S.A. was founded in 1993 and was created to operate in the areas of commercial information services, and databases in Chile and abroad. Later, in 1995 TWS started the division of commercial collections. The service that TWS provides includes the domestic and international debt collection, outsourcing and receivables management.
